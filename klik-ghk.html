Is GHK-Cu safe?

Yes. GHK-Cu is a naturally occurring peptide already found in the human body. It has a strong safety profile and is widely used in cosmetic and dermatologic treatments.

Does it replace my skincare routine?

No GHK-Cu complements your routine. While your skincare products work on the surface, GHK-Cu supports deeper repair, collagen remodeling, and cellular rejuvenation beneath the skin.

How long does it last?

The duration and results depend on your clinicians recommended dosage and usage schedule. Each individuals response can vary based on skin goals and consistency of application.

Absolutely. GHK-Cu is effective and safe for all skin types, and men often see significant improvements in firmness, texture, and skin quality.
