Summary

Justin Jones, a resident of Waco, Texas, has been participating in a weight loss program for three months and one week, shedding 55 pounds. Despite having tried numerous diets before, he found previous plans lacking in personalization and motivation. The program he joined, however, offers personalized coaching and a focus on psychological aspects, which he believes is crucial for long-term success. He credits his coach, Sean, for understanding his goals and providing the necessary support and motivation. Jones emphasizes that the program's success is not solely due to medication but also due to the personal touch and the effort he puts in, ensuring he won't revert to his old habits.
